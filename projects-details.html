Welcome to a life where you 'Live Refined' as life isn't meant to be spent but savoured and enjoyed.

Discover the ultimate refinement & Live Well

A place for those who want more out of life, who want to be connected to the city but remain untouched by its chaos. The ones who want amenities to not only just relax but also get inspired. Lodha Adrina is designed for those who don’t just want a place to live but a residence that promises good health, happiness and absolute well-being.

Because we were meant to live with nature

At Lodha Adrina, nature isn’t relegated to pots and corners but is allowed to thrive. Swathes of greenery wend their way from the gardens to the indoor spaces to the rooftop. At the heart of all this are the private forest, a place of serenity, solitude and ethereal beauty.

An everyday life, elevated

A biophillic landscape hosting multiple amenities on the ground level and it follows you 150 feet above the ground. The rooftop hosts a winding lagoon pool overlooking enchanting treetops and an exclusive clubhouse aloft the building amongst others. Here the standard of everyday activities is sky- high.

Lifestyle at Lodha Acenza

Whether you are indoors or out, Acenza’s various amenities ensure you make the most of your lifestyle through the year.

Take the family down to the swimming pools for a day of fun or simply let your little ones run loose in the designated play areas and connect with your neighbours in the gourmet cafe.

Lodha Adrina RERA

The World Towers - MahaRERA registration no.

The World Towers : The project has been registered via MahaRERA registration numbers: P51900008345, P51900008962 and is available on the website https://maharera.mahaonline.gov.in under registered projects.
RERA is an iconic act that aims to bring transparency and efficiency in the real estate sector, protect consumers, and fast track dispute resolution. It is an act that promotes good governance in the market, which helps customers get more information about projects and reduces the risk associated with the purchase.

The MahaRERA Registration Number is the mark of integrity, and all new Lodha Group projects are RERA compliant; see a list of our RERA projects here.
