Campus Case Study Challenge

Soar for More is an initiative to foster innovation through inspiring solutions to challenges that the real estate industry encounters on a regular basis. It is primarily an opportunity for the cream of the crop from premier management institutes in India to inspire and ideate through business concepts.

The case is centered on a functional challenge that the participants need to provide innovative solutions to, while keeping a pragmatic outlook of the same. The winning teams receive a PPI for the prestigious Lodha Global Management Program.
