Shaping the future of work

As India’s Largest real estate developer, and with our vision of Building a Better Life, Lodha has a responsibility, not only to the
individuals who live and work in our developments, but also to India-to support the nation’s development and help the country
thrive. It is estimated that the real estate sector currently contributes ~18% of total employment in India and that by 2047 it will
contribute 15.5% to total economic output* (almost double what it is today).

We also have the opportunity, scale and expertise to have the largest impact. As we work towards this goal of building a better
life, we are committed to making a lasting, positive impact on both the environment and on society.
