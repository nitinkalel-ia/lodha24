Building for the Future

As India’s largest real estate developer, Lodha has a responsibility, not only to the individuals who live and work in our developments, but also to support the nation’s development and help the country thrive.

Our aim is to grow in a manner that positively contributes to both – our planet and our people. This approach has helped us to be ranked amongst the top companies in leading global sustainability benchmarks^.

View our Integrated Report

Environmental Impact

Our Environmental Impact priorities are to lead India’s transition towards Net Zero , by delivering our own net zero targets and helping other organisations to follow suit and develop assets that are resilient to the changing environment, through defined focus areas under Decarbonisation and Resilience.

We believe that smart, sustainable construction and take proactive measures to mitigate the impact of climate risks and develop assets that are resilient to the changing environment.

Social Impact

Lodha is committed to philanthropic initiatives and other interventions which aid India’s development: socially and environmentally. For Social Impact our current areas of focus are Women's Empowerment and Education.

Unnati, Lodha’s flagship women’s economic empowerment initiative, champions and enables increased female participation in the formal workforce.

Lodha school prepares students for academic excellence through global curriculum – across IGCSE, ICSE, CBSE, SSC & ISC curricula.
